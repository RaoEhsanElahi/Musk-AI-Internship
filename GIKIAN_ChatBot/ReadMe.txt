GIKIAN Chatbot: An AI-powered University Query Assistant
Introduction:
        The GIKI Chatbot is an intelligent conversational agent designed to assist students, faculty, and staff with a seamless experience of accessing information related to our esteemed university, Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute (GIKI). Harnessing the power of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Deep Learning, this chatbot empowers users to ask questions and get prompt and accurate responses, making it a valuable resource for our university community. 

Features:
        Query Answering: The GIKI Chatbot is equipped to handle a wide range of questions regarding various aspects of the university, including academic programs, faculty information, campus facilities, events, and much more. With a user-friendly interface, users can easily interact with the chatbot to seek the information they need.
        Past Conversation Storage: The GIKI Chatbot takes user privacy seriously. All conversations with the chatbot are stored securely in a database, allowing users to access their previous interactions whenever required. Additionally, this historical data enables the chatbot to personalize future interactions based on the user's preferences and needs.
        There is also API generated and a Frontend UI created on react.
